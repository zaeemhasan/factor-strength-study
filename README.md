Signal Engine v2.5 is a Python-based quantitative framework for analyzing financial signals and combining them into trading strategies, featuring multi-source data integration from Yahoo Finance, FRED, and Financial Modeling Prep. It supports regime-aware modeling, Information Coefficient (IC) validation, and thematic universe analysis, with specific modules for short interest and credit market signals.








What is a signal?
A signal is any measurable data point with a statistically repeatable relationship to future price or probability movement.
